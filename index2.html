Our Objectives

The esports and Gaming club is the youngest club of IIT Indore. It is by nature very different from other clubs in terms of the club culture, and that comes with its own set of perks and responsibilities. The club members are all determined to leave their mark on the gaming culture of IITI and beyond. Our aim is to promote happy lifestyle far from depression and hectic schedule faced by students.
